About Surya Tech Solutions

Professional Telecom & Fiber Solutions

Surya Tech Solutions is a multi-division technical enterprise operating across Telecom Infrastructure, Renewable Energy, Industrial Contract Manufacturing, and Skill Development.

Our Andhra Pradesh operations are executed under RS Infocom Solutions. We are officially registered under MSME and affiliated with NASSCOM and Cisco, reinforcing compliance, technical standards, and industry recognition.

Can you handle large-scale infrastructure projects ?

Yes. We execute end-to-end telecom and optical fiber deployments including FTTx, CAT6 cabling, and maintenance services with structured field teams and supervisory control.

How is your solar division different ?

Under SUNGLARE SOLAR, we provide structured residential and commercial installations with proper site assessment, system design, and professional commissioning.

Do you partner with colleges for training programs ?

Yes. We collaborate with institutions through MoUs, AICTE internship programs, workshops, and campus recruitment drives to bridge the industry-academia gap.

What makes Surya Tech Solutions different ?

Our strength lies in combining execution, manufacturing support, and skill development under one structured organization. We are MSME registered, associated with NASSCOM, and aligned with industry standards.
